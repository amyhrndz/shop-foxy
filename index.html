Shopping small is easy with Foxy

Support small businesses you love with the convenience you crave.

Foxy connects smart shoppers with their favorite local businesses. Choosing Foxy as your shipping or pickup method supports small business, gets you your good faster, and is a green option!


How does Foxy work?

Intuitively simple, Foxy is integrated into the delivery options of your favorite small businesses. Select Foxy at checkout and you’ll get free 2-day shipping!

1

Create an account with Foxy

Click Get Started to enter your details. We take your privacy seriously.

2

Shop your favorite stores at checkout

Visit the website of any Foxy retailer and shop as you normally would.

3

Choose Foxy at checkout

It's included with the payment options. Your account info is already stored with us.

4

Receive your purchase

Get your items quickly and securely! Same-day courier delivery may apply.

Quick delivery, secure payments with Foxy

Once you've created an account, every Foxy checkout becomes a breeze. Confirm your shipping address and that's it. Your package is on its way!

Go Green!

Get easy shipping from all of your favorite small businesses. We’re dedicated to keeping Earth clean. If the destination of your goods is local to the store where you bought it, Foxy delivers by bike or you have the option to pick up the purchase yourself.

And if you need to ship further, don’t worry, we’ve pledged to offset all carbon emissions produced by shipping so your Foxy experience is always carbon-neutral. Turns out, it’s pretty easy being green!
